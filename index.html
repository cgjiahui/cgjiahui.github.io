Bio

 I obtained B.S in Hefei University in 2020 and Master's degree in School of Computer Science and Information Engineering in Hefei University of Technology in 2023. My supervisors were Dr. Wenming Wu and Prof. Liping Zheng. During my graduate studies, I also had the privilege of collaborating with Prof. Ligang Liu on the research project WallPlan.
My research interests are centered around developing intelligent algorithms to comprehend and model both 2D and 3D environments. Currently, I am keenly interested in AIGC on 2D and 3D. .
